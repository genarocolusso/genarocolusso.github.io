Alone in a strange place, trapped, you discover yourself while searching for a meaning for everything you do.

Your actions are precisely calculated and you want to escape this place to be where you once belonged.
Are you prepared?

Acid, Enemies and traps. Prepare to explore an vicious place or die trying

Thiago Tunes, Luis Felipe Kemmerich, Matheus Tanuri, Genaro Colusso, Vinicius Capiotti, Vinicius Ibaldo

Clavus Project

First version of Clavus was created in about 20 hours during the first Game Jam of Federal University of Santa Maria on october 21st 2016
